In the summer of 2020, I got into the pokemon trading cards. This was accompanied by a recent wave at the time, where they started gaining huge popularity due to a nostalgia wave. I started collecting some fake cards, not knowing they were fake at the time and I was really into it. When I came back from vacation, I got really into collecting them, having learned how to get my hands on the real trading cards. As my pokemon card binder got bigger, I found more trading card collections, such as sports cards of all different varieties and Yu-Gi-Oh. I started becoming really interested in old, rare and exotic cards that I could get my hands into, including football cards from the 80’s and pokemon cards from the 90’s. Today, I have amassed three different binders, full of cards that I gathered from 3 and a half years of collecting, with my prized possession being a limited japanese magi magi dark magician girl at the back of my phone case.

The types of cards I collect

  • Pokemon
  • Yu-Gi-Oh
  • Sports Cards
  • Magic The Gathering
  • K-pop Photocards
